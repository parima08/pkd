The Birth of Prophet

Shrimad Rajchandra was born on November 9th, 1867 in Vavania, Gujarat a small port near Morvi in Saurashtra. He was born on the night of the full moon (Karthik Poonam), a foreshadowing of his contribution to humanity. Just as the moon brings light in the darkness of night, Shrimad Rajchandra would bring knowledge and awakening to a sleeping world.

He was born to Ravjibhai and Devbai, a devout couple who served the community with much love.
